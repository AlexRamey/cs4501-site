Home Page (iteration 3)

As a shopper in need of Christmas present ideas, I want to see some ‘hot items’ displayed on the home page

Shopping Cart Page

As a busy shopper, I want to be able to add items to my shopping cart and view them when I come back to the site later

Profile Page (iteration 3)

As a buyer, I wish to be able to see a list of my past purchases

As a seller, I wish to be able to see a list of my past sold items and items which are currently for sale

Category Specific Home Pages

As an entertainment enthusiast, I wish to peruse the different categories on the site and see the different products listed

General Search Page (iteration 3)

As a user, I want to be able to search among the products/vendors

Product Detail Page (iteration 3)

As a careful shopper I wish to see the rating of a vendor and the other items he is selling

Checkout Page 

As a merchant, I wish to have my payments processed and verified by a third party

Account (iteration 4)

As a seller, I wish to be able to create an account on this site to list my items for sale
As a buyer or seller, I wish to be able to log into my account on this site to check activity
As a buyer or seller, I wish to be able to log out of my account on this site so that people I share my computer with cannot see my listings
As a user, I wish to see a personalized message on the home page after I log in
As a seller, I wish to be able to create a new listing if I have something to sell
As a seller, I wish to be able to update a listing in case any information changes
As a seller, I wish to be able to remove/delete a listing in case the product no longer exists anymore
As a seller, I wish to be able to see my most recently listed products on home page (if I am logged in)

Search (iteration 5)

As a buyer, I want to be able to search all possible listings that I can buy
As a seller, I want to be able to create a listing for a product I want to sell
